üíª Part 1: PC (Mouse & Keyboard) - The Gold Standard

The PC meta in 2026 focuses on "Movement Hygiene" and minimizing finger travel time for abilities.

  1. Movement & Interaction
    • WASD: Standard movement.
    • Shift (Hold): Walk (Essential for silent movement).
    • Ctrl: Crouch.
    • Space / Mouse Wheel Down: Jump.
      • Pro Tip: Binding jump to Mouse Wheel Down is the 2026 standard for easier "Bunny Hopping" and hitting perfect movement resets.
    • F (or E): Use Object / Interact / Plant / Defuse.
    • G: Drop weapon.
    • Y: Inspect weapon (Essential for those 2026 dual-wield skins).
  2. Combat & Weapons
    • Left Click: Fire.
    • Right Click: Aim Down Sights (ADS) / Alt Fire.
    • R: Reload.
    • 1, 2, 3: Primary, Secondary, and Melee weapons.
    • 4 (or 5): Equip Spike.
  3. The "Ability" Layout (The 2026 Shift)

    Modern pros have moved away from default keys to keep their fingers on WASD at all times:

    • Ability 1: Mouse Button 4 (Side button).
    • Ability 2: Q or E.
    • Ability 3: C or Mouse Button 5.
    • Ultimate: X or F.

Part 1: Essential Advanced Settings

Activate these pro features that beginners ignore for competitive advantage

Horizontal Swipe Acceleration

Recommended: ON

Smooths out your camera movement and makes 180° turns faster. Essential for close-quarter combat.

Peek & Fire

Recommended: ON

Set "Lean Mode" to Hold. Tap to peek, release to un-peek. Critical for cover-based gunfights.

Scope Mode

Recommended: Hold

Faster than Tap. Scope opens instantly when touching button, closes when releasing.

Auto-Adjust Graphics

Recommended: OFF

Maintain consistent frame rate. Even 5 FPS drops can ruin recoil control in intense fights.

Quick Weapon Switch

Recommended: ON

Swap between primary and secondary weapons instantly without reaching for inventory.

In 2026, 99% of pro players use "Always On" Gyroscope for recoil control

Why Use Gyroscope?

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common PUBG control questions

Should I use Gyroscope in 2026?

Absolutely! Gyroscope provides the most precise recoil control possible. It takes about a week to learn but doubles your combat effectiveness. Start with low sensitivity (200%) and gradually increase.

How to fix "Joystick Stuck" feeling?

Place your joystick slightly higher than default position and set its size to 85%. Also enable "Fixed Joystick" in settings for consistent movement.

Best button opacity for competitive play?

Set opacity to 30-40%. Buttons should be barely visible but still usable. Critical buttons like fire can be at 50% for muscle memory.

How to practice 4-finger claw effectively?

Start in Training Mode for 30 minutes daily. Focus on one finger at a time. Week 1: Master movement+aiming. Week 2: Add peeking. Week 3: Add crouch-jumping. Week 4: Combine all in Arcade matches.

Why does my 8x scope drift after update?

This is a known bug in Version 4.2 for 90Hz+ devices. Reduce 8x Gyro sensitivity to 90%, recalibrate gyro on flat surface, and avoid using 8x on moving vehicles.

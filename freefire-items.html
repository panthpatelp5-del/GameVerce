Grenades (Frag & Flash)

Combat
Frag Grenade

Has a "cook" timer. Hold the button until the timer is at 1.5 seconds, then throw for an instant explosion.

Flashbang

Landmines

Tactical

Set these near vending machines, loot boxes, or at the top of stairs.

Tactical Use: Place a landmine inside a dropped Gloo Wall. When the wall expires or is destroyed, the enemy rushing you will step on the mine.

High-Tech Gadgets (2026 Meta)

Tactical Gadgets that fit into a special slot in your backpack

The Jammer

Meta

Equip this to become "ghost-like" on enemy radar. Blocks UAVs, Clu's tracking, and Moco's tags.

Why Use It

If you are a flanker, this is mandatory. Enemies will look at their mini-map and think you aren't there.

Counter

Useful against scanning characters like Clu and Moco.

Loot Radar

Utility

Automatically pings high-level loot (Level 3 vests, rare guns) within 75 meters.

Why Use It

Speeds up your looting process so you can focus on fighting instead of searching every room.

Strategy

Best for players who want to gear up quickly and engage early.

Horizonline (Self-Revive)

Game-Changer

Allows you to revive yourself when knocked down without needing a teammate.

Why Use It

Can only be used once per match. Save it for crucial moments.

Pro Tip

If you are knocked, crawl behind cover before using to avoid being finished off.

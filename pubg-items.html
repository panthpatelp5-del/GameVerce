Tactical Gear (2026 Meta)

Dedicated tactical slot items that provide strategic advantages

Drone

Scouting

Fly up to 200m away to scout buildings and can pick up loot remotely.

Why Use It

Essential for safe reconnaissance and grabbing loot from dangerous areas.

Counter

Can be shot down. Use in short bursts to avoid detection.

Blue Chip Detector

Intel

Scans for enemies within 50m every 8 seconds. Cannot detect Jammer Pack users.

Why Use It

Perfect for holding buildings and checking for nearby threats.

Strategy

Best for defensive players and squads holding strategic positions.

Emergency Pickup

Mobility

Orders a plane to extract your team and drop you into the safe zone.

Why Use It

Essential for edge-of-map players caught in blue zone.

Pro Tip

Call pickup from cover as the plane is visible and audible to enemies.

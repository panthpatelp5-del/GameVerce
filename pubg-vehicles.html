The "Vehicle-as-Cover"
Hard Cover Tactics
Defensive

When caught in an open field, don't just jump out. Use the vehicle as mobile cover.

Pro Move:

1. Pull handbrake and drift sideways to enemy
2. Vehicle stops horizontally to enemy
3. Pop tires on side facing enemy (lowers chassis)
4. Use as bulletproof cover

Offensive

• Use passenger seats (2, 3, or 4)
• Lean out with SMG (low recoil while moving)
• M416 works but SMG better
• Coordinate with driver for stable platform

Frequently Asked Questions
Expert Knowledge
Q: What do smoke colors mean?

A: Grey Smoke = 50% health. Black Smoke/Fire = below 20%, will explode soon. Jump immediately!

Q: Can Gas Cans have other uses?

A: Yes! In 2026, you can: 1) Pour fuel to create fire traps, 2) Throw and shoot for explosions, 3) Create environmental hazards.

Q: Can I repair vehicles?

A: Not HP repair, but Repair Kits fix popped tires and engine stalls on certain maps.

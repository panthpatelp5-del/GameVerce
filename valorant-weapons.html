üÜï 1. The Newest Addition: The Bandit

The Bandit is the first sidearm added since the game's launch, designed to bridge the gap between the Ghost and the Sheriff. It is a "budget cannon" for players who trust their aim.

  • Price: 600 Credits.
  • Magazine: 8 Rounds (24 Reserve).
  • The "Lethality" Rule: Unlike the Ghost, the Bandit one-taps enemies with Light Armor (125 HP) to the head at up to 30 meters.
  • Best For: Force-buys and Anti-eco rounds. It allows you to save 200 credits compared to a Sheriff while still maintaining "one-tap" potential against players who skipped heavy shields.

  • ADS Fixes: Patch 12.00 fixed Aim-Down-Sights (ADS) animation bugs for almost half the roster (Clove, Jett, Iso, etc.), making the transition from hip-fire to scoping feel instant.

üí° 5. Economy Strategy: How to Buy in 2026

  • The "Thrifty" Bandit: If you win the pistol round, don't just buy a Spectre. If the enemy is likely to "force" light shields, a Bandit allows you to save money for a full Operator buy by Round 3.
  • The "Half-Buy" Outlaw: If your team has ~3,300 credits, buy an Outlaw + Light Shield. It is mathematically the most efficient way to punish enemies who are on a full-buy with Heavy Shields.
  • The "Pocket Guardian": Agents like Chamber and Iso now use the Bandit as their primary eco tool, as their abilities provide the "shielding" or "mobility" needed to make the 8-round magazine work.

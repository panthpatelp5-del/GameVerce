üí• Part 3: The 2026 "Ultrakill" Crossover Weapons

Introduced in late 2025/early 2026, these weapons introduced "Mechanical" gameplay loops to Palworld.

  1. Marksman Revolver (The Ricoshot)
    • Unique Mechanic: You can throw a Coin into the air. If you shoot the coin, the bullet ricochets into the nearest enemy's weak spot for critical damage.
    • Ammo: Handgun Ammo.
  2. Core Eject Shotgun
    • Unique Mechanic: It doesn't use traditional shells; it's powered by heat. Instead of throwing a Pal Sphere, your secondary fire launches a Heat Core that explodes.
    • Combo: If you shoot your Heat Core mid-air with the Marksman Revolver, it creates a massive explosion that can clear entire Syndicate camps.

üß† Part 6: Pro Weapon Strategies

  1. The "Mercy Hit" Mechanic

    In 2026, if you use a weapon with the Mercy Hit enchant (or use the Ring of Mercy), you cannot kill a Pal‚Äîit will always stay at 1 HP. This is mandatory for catching Rare/Shiny Pals without accidentally killing them.

  2. Schematic Farming

    Don't just craft the "Gray" versions of weapons. Hunt Alpha Bosses to find Legendary Schematics (Gold).

    • Legendary Assault Rifle: Dropped by Blazamut.
    • Legendary Rocket Launcher: Dropped by Jetragon.
    • Stat Boost: A Legendary weapon deals roughly 2.5x more damage than the standard version.
  3. The "Pal-Weapon" Synergy

    Some Pals are weapons.

    • Tanzee: Can use an AK-47 (Partner Skill).
    • Lifmunk: Sits on your head and uses a Submachine Gun while you fire your own weapon.
    • Foxparks: Becomes a literal Flamethrower in your hands.

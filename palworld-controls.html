üíª Part 1: PC (Mouse & Keyboard) - The Tactical Choice

PC remains the preferred platform for high-level base building and precise sniping.

  1. Basic Movement & Interaction
    • WASD: Standard movement.
    • Spacebar: Jump. (Double-tap while flying a mount to ascend).
    • Shift (Hold): Sprint. (Drains Stamina).
    • Ctrl: Roll / Dodge. (Provides "i-frames" to avoid boss attacks).
    • C: Crouch / Slide. (Slide down hills for a massive speed boost).
    • F (Hold): Interact / Build / Partner Skill.
    • V: Pick up a Pal (manually throw them at a workstation to assign them).
  2. Combat & Pal Management
    • Left Click: Attack / Use Tool.
    • Right Click: Aim / Cancel Pal Sphere throw.
    • Q: Throw Pal Sphere. (Tap to throw, Hold to aim).
    • E: Summon / Recall Pal.
    • 1 & 3: Cycle through your current Pal Party.
    • 2: Cycle through different types of Pal Spheres (Mega, Giga, Legendary).
    • 4: Pal Command Wheel. (Set your Pals to "Attack Aggressively" or "Focus on Same Enemy").
    • R: Reload Weapon.

üì± Part 3: Mobile (2026 Official Release)

The 2026 mobile version features a "Contextual HUD" that changes based on whether you are at your base or in combat.

  • Virtual Joystick: Left side for movement.
  • Dynamic Action Button: A large button on the right that switches between "Mine," "Chop," and "Attack" based on what you are looking at.
  • Swipe Gestures: Swipe the Pal icon at the bottom-center to quickly switch between your active Pals.
  • Auto-Fire: A toggleable setting in the 2026 Mobile menu that automatically shoots your weapon when your crosshair is over a target (highly recommended for touch users).

  1. Building "Grid Snapping"

    In the 2026 "Home Sweet Home" update, a new "Snap Toggle" was added.

    • PC: Press Alt while building to disable snapping for free-form placement.
    • Console: Press the View/Select Button to toggle between "Grid" and "Fine-Tune" mode.
  2. Sensitivity & Deadzones

    If you are playing the 1.0 version, check your Camera Acceleration settings.

    • Recommendation: Set "Camera Acceleration" to 0 for a more consistent feel in shooters.
    • Deadzones: If your character "drifts" on console, increase the Deadzone to 0.15 in the Options menu.
  3. The "Work Slave" Shortcut

    You can now customize a hotkey for the Monitoring Stand. Instead of walking to the stand, you can press a dedicated key (default K on PC) to open the Work Preference menu from anywhere within your base limits.

üß† Part 5: Pro Control Tips

  1. The "Gilde-Slide" Combo:

    Sprint down a hill, hit C to slide, and immediately press Space to deploy your Glider. You will maintain the slide's momentum, traveling 3x faster than flying.

  2. Instant Dismount:

    While riding a Pal, you can quickly dismount by pressing E (or LB/L1). If you do this while jumping, you'll perform a "Double Jump" off the Pal's back.

  3. Sphere Aiming:

    If you find it hard to hit small Pals, go to Settings > Gameplay and enable "Aim Assist." In the 1.0 update, this now also affects Pal Sphere trajectories!

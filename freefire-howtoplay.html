The "Steep Glide" Technique

  • The Goal: Land before everyone else.
  • The Trick: Jump when the plane is about 600 meters from your target. Point your joystick straight down and use the Falcon pet (if you have it) to increase diving speed.
  • Pro Tip: If you see 3+ squads landing in your spot, veer off to a nearby smaller cluster of houses. A bad gun is better than no gun.

üî• Phase 3: Combat Tactics (The Pro Moves)

Winning a gunfight isn't just about tapping the fire button. Use these "2026 Meta" movement tricks.

The "Drag Headshot"

Basic Pro Skill

As you fire, quickly flick your right thumb upward. This pulls the "Aim Assist" from the chest to the head.

SMGs

Fast, short drag

ARs

Slow, steady drag

The "Crouch-Uncrouch" Peek

Advanced

When hiding behind a rock or window, don't just stand up and shoot.

Why? It resets your weapon's recoil and makes it nearly impossible for snipers to hit your head.

The "Third-Party" Rule

Expert

If you hear two teams fighting nearby, DO NOT rush in immediately.

  • Wait for the "Kill Feed" to show 2-3 people have died.
  • That is when both teams are low on HP and reloading. Jump in then to clean up both squads for easy kills.

üèîÔ∏è Phase 4: The End-Game (The Final Circle)

The circle is tiny, 10 people are left, and everyone has Gloo Walls. This is where champions are made.

High Ground is King

Always try to take the highest hill or the tallest building. Shooting downwards is 50% easier than shooting upwards because you have a clearer view of the enemy's head.

Gloo Wall Economy

In the final circle, don't save your walls. Use them to create a "path" to the next safe zone.

Double Wall Technique Pro Tip

Place two walls, one slightly behind the other. If the enemy has a "M82B" (which shoots through walls), the second wall will save you.

Patience is Key

In a small circle, the first person to shoot reveals their position to everyone else. Let the other teams fight each other, and only engage when you are forced to or when there is only one team left.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the most important skill for beginners?

Mastering movement and positioning. Good positioning can win fights even with worse aim. Always take high ground and use cover effectively.

How many Gloo Walls should I carry?

Minimum 3, ideal 5-6 for end-game. In competitive play, having extra Gloo Walls can be the difference between winning and losing.

What's the best weapon combo for 2026?

SCAR (long range) + MP40 (close range) or M82B (sniper) + Vector (SMG). The meta changes with updates, but always have one long and one short range weapon.

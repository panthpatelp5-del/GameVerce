Separating fashion facts from battlefield fiction

Do skins give stats or advantages?

NO. PUBG is strictly anti-pay-to-win. A gold M416 does exactly the same damage as a default one. Skins are purely cosmetic.

Do skins make your hitbox bigger?

Visually yes, technically no. If you wear a giant dinosaur suit, you're much easier for snipers to spot from 300m away, but your actual hitbox remains the same.

Is the Ghillie Suit a skin?

NO. The Ghillie Suit is an in-game item found only in Airdrops. It cannot be purchased or acquired through any skin system.

Are collab skins worth it?

Subjectively. Anime collabs (Jujutsu Kaisen, Dragon Ball) change hairstyles and voices. Meme skins (KFC Colonel) are for psychological warfare. Value depends on personal preference.

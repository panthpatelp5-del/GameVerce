Part 3: Mobile Touch Controls

Master the 2026 "Touch Overhaul" for iOS & Android

Standard

Joystick Mode

Pros
  • Familiar floating joystick
  • Easy to learn
  • Good for casual play
Cons
  • Limited screen space
  • Less precise than split
  • Harder for complex builds

Pro Tip: Adjust joystick size and position to match your thumb reach. Keep it large enough for comfort but small enough to not block view.

Advanced

Split Controls Mode

Setup: Crosshair in screen center, separate movement/attack zones

Advantage: PC-like precision, better for competitive play and detailed building

Part 4: 2026 Meta Settings

Optimize your settings for the current meta

Field of View (FOV)

Set to 80-90. Default (60) feels too narrow. Higher FOV lets you see mobs sneaking up from sides.

Recommended: 85 META

Auto-Jump

Turn it OFF. Auto-jump can be dangerous when building near edges or lava. Manual control is better.

Recommended: OFF PRO

Mace Control Trick

Map "Jump" to accessible button (LB/L1) so you can aim while jumping for perfect mace smash timing.

How do I fly down in Creative mode?

Hold the Sneak button (Left Shift on PC, Circle on PlayStation, B on Xbox) while flying to descend. Double-tap jump to ascend.

What's the best sensitivity for building vs PvP?

Building: 30-50% for precision placement.
PvP: 60-80% for quick 180-degree turns.
Hybrid: 50-70% for balanced gameplay.

How do I use quick emotes in multiplayer?

D-Pad Left opens emote menu. 2026 update added "Quick-Emote" slots that can be bound to specific buttons for instant communication during gameplay.

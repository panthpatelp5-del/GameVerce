üåÖ Phase 1: The First 20 Minutes (The "Golden Start")

Your first day determines if you'll spend the night fighting for your life or sleeping soundly.

Punch a Tree

  • Step 1: Collect about 20 logs
  • Step 2: Open your inventory, turn logs into Planks
  • Step 3: Craft a Crafting Table and place it down immediately
  • Pro Tip: Look for Oak trees first - they're the most common and drop apples

The "Wooden Age" is 10 Seconds Long

  • Only craft a Wooden Pickaxe - nothing else
  • Dig straight into a hillside to get Cobblestone
  • Immediately craft Stone Tools (Sword, Pickaxe, Axe, Shovel)
  • Throw the wooden pickaxe in a furnace as fuel‚Äîyou won't need it again

Find the "New" Babies

  • In 2026, keep an eye out for the redesigned Wolf Pups and Kittens
  • They have improved AI and can be tamed earlier to help you hunt for food
  • Wolf Pups protect you from hostile mobs
  • Kittens scare away Creepers and Phantoms

The Bed is Mandatory

  • Kill 3 sheep or find a village with a shepherd
  • You MUST sleep to skip the night on your first day
  • If you don't, Phantoms will start spawning by the third night
  • In 2026, Phantoms are more aggressive and faster

First Day Priority Checklist

Priority Item Why It's Important
1 Wood (20+ logs) Foundation for all early game items
2 Cobblestone Tools Mining, defense, and resource gathering
3 Coal (10+) Lighting, cooking, and furnace fuel
4 Bed & Shelter Skip nights, avoid mob spawns
5 Food Source Wheat farm or animal pen for sustainability

üè∞ Phase 2: Settling & Automation (The Mid-Game)

Once you have a stone base, stop playing "manually" and start using the 2026 tech.

Build the "Crafter" ASAP

The Crafter is the game-changer of 2026. It allows for auto-crafting of items.

Game Changer

The "Pale Garden" Expedition

Travel until you find the Pale Garden (the gray, eerie biome).

Advanced

Villager Trading 2.0

Villagers are still the best way to get Diamond Gear in 2026.

Pro Tip

Crafter Setup

  • The Setup: Connect a Hopper filled with Iron Ingots to a Crafter
  • Redstone Clock: Use two observers facing each other for timing
  • Result: It will automatically spit out Iron Chestplates or Tools while you sleep
  • Advanced: Connect multiple crafters for complex item production chains

Pale Garden Mission

  • Locate: Find a Creaking Heart block (glows faintly)
  • Silk Touch: Use Silk-Touch to mine it without breaking
  • Golem: Bring it to your base to summon your own "Creaking" golem
  • Protection: The golem defends your home from mobs and other players

Villager Strategy

  • Fletcher: Turn into Master for Emeralds from sticks
  • Librarian: Use Emeralds to buy Enchanted Books (Mending, Fortune III)
  • Farmers: Best for early game emerald income
  • Zombie Cure: Cure zombie villagers for massive discounts

üåå Phase 3: Dimensional Travel (Nether & The End)

To "beat" the game, you must leave the Overworld and conquer other dimensions.

The Nether Preparation

Essential
  • Gold Armor: Full set to prevent Piglins from attacking
  • Fire Resistance: Potions or enchanted golden apples
  • Good Weapons: Diamond sword with Sharpness III minimum
  • Building Blocks: At least 2 stacks of cobblestone
2026 Update: The Nether is more vertical; use Striders or the new Nautilus-link to travel across lava lakes quickly.

Blaze Rods & Ender Pearls

Advanced
Blaze Hunting
  • Find Nether Fortress
  • Build safe platform
  • Use bow from distance
  • Collect 12+ Blaze Rods
Pearl Gathering
  • Trade Gold with Piglins
  • Kill Endermen safely
  • Build 2-block high roof
  • Get 12+ Ender Pearls

The Dragon Fight Strategy

Expert

The Bed Method: Still the fastest way to win in 2026

  • Wait for Dragon to perch on center fountain
  • Place bed next to fountain (but not too close!)
  • Right-click bed to explode it
  • Repeat 4-6 times
  • Shoot crystals on pillars first
Warning: Stand back when beds explode! They deal massive damage to you too.

The game doesn't end when the credits roll. This is where true mastery begins.

The Mace & Elytra Combo

The ultimate 2026 combat meta is the Mace. If you jump from a height using an Elytra (wings) and hit an enemy with a Mace just before landing, you deal massive "smash" damage and take zero fall damage.

Damage Output 40+

One-hit kill most mobs with perfect timing

Height Required 4+ blocks

Minimum drop height for smash effect

Trial Chambers

Look for the new Trial Chambers (large underground copper structures with unique loot).

Spear Strategy New Weapon

Use the new Spear (2026 weapon) to keep the "Breeze" mobs at a distance. They drop Trial Keys which unlock "Vaults" filled with Enchanted Golden Apples and Diamond blocks.

Reward System Procedural

These are procedurally generated dungeons with increasing difficulty. Complete waves of challenges for better rewards.

Follow these rules religiously to avoid common death traps.

Never Dig Straight Down

You will hit lava. In 2026, the new "Mega-Caves" mean you might fall 100 blocks into a lake of fire.

Always Carry a Water Bucket

It's your "parachute" (landing in water negates fall damage) and your "fire extinguisher."

Light Level 0 = Mob Spawn

Mobs only spawn when it is pitch black. Place torches every 7‚Äì8 blocks to keep your base 100% safe.

Food Over Everything

Never let your hunger drop below 8 points. Starvation damage ignores armor.

Inventory Management

Always carry: Water bucket, food, torches, pickaxe, sword, and building blocks.

Coordinate Book

Write down coordinates of everything important: base, villages, portals, mineshafts.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best food source for beginners?

Bread from wheat farming. It's renewable, easy to automate, and provides decent saturation. Set up a 9x9 farm with water in the center as soon as you have seeds.

How do I find diamonds efficiently?

Mine at Y=-58 level in 1x2 tunnels spaced 3 blocks apart. Use Fortune III pickaxe when you get it. Bring plenty of torches, food, and always have an escape route.

What's the most important enchantment?

Mending. It repairs your gear using XP orbs. Combine it with Unbreaking III and your tools/weapons/armor will last forever. Get it from Librarian villagers or fishing.

How do I deal with the Warden?

Don't fight it. The Warden is designed to be avoided. Use wool to muffle sound, sneak everywhere, and use projectiles to distract it. If it detects you, RUN and don't look back.

üïí Phase 1: The Foundations (The Round Loop)

Every match is a 5v5 tactical battle. The first team to reach 13 rounds (or 7 in Swiftplay) wins.

  1. The Buy Phase (45s):

    You start with 800 credits. Buy weapons, shields, and abilities.

    • 2026 Meta: The Bandit (600 creds) is the new "Pistol Round" king. It rewards headshots more than the Ghost but is more forgiving than the Sheriff.
  2. The Objective:
    • Attackers: Plant the Spike at a site (A, B, or C) and defend it for 45 seconds until it detonates.
    • Defenders: Prevent the plant or defuse the Spike (7 seconds to defuse).
  3. The Half-Time Swap:

    After 12 rounds, teams switch sides. Your economy resets, so spend everything in the final round of the first half!

üí∞ Phase 2: Mastering the "Eco" (Economy Management)

You don't just buy a rifle every round. You must manage your team's money to ensure you are never "out-gunned" for long.

  • Save (Eco) Round: Your team has less than 2,000 credits. Buy nothing or just a cheap pistol (Classic/Shorty) so you can afford a full loadout next round.
  • Force Buy: You lost the last round but have enough for SMGs and Light Shields. This is a high-risk play to catch the enemy off-guard.
  • Full Buy: You have 3,900+ credits. Buy a Vandal/Phantom, Heavy Shields, and all your abilities.
  • The "Bonus" Round: If you win the second round after winning the pistol, keep your SMGs! Even if you lose against the enemy's full rifles, you‚Äôve "saved" money for a better buy later.

üéØ Phase 3: The Golden Rules of Combat

Valorant is not a "run-and-gun" shooter. If you move while shooting, your bullets will fly wild.

  1. Stop, Then Shoot:

    You must be perfectly still to be accurate. Use "Counter-Strafing" (tap the opposite movement key) to come to a dead stop instantly before clicking.

  2. Crosshair Placement:

    Never look at the floor. Keep your crosshair at head height where you expect an enemy to appear. This minimizes the distance you have to move your mouse to get a kill.

  3. The 2-Shot Burst:

    Don't spray your gun (holding the trigger). Fire in bursts of 2‚Äì3 bullets. In 2026, the "Tap Efficiency" buff makes tapping more viable than ever for long-range fights.

  4. Audio is Intel:

    Footsteps are loud. If you are near an enemy, hold Shift to Walk. If they hear you coming, they will have their crosshair waiting for your head.

ü§ù Phase 4: Role-Based Teamwork

You aren't just a soldier; you are an Agent with a specific job.

  • Duelists (Jett, Phoenix, Waylay): Your job is to go in first. Use your mobility to "create space" and take the first fight.
  • Initiators (Sova, Skye, Tejo): Your job is to find the enemy. Use your drones and scans to tell your Duelists exactly where to aim.
  • Controllers (Clove, Omen, Viper): Your job is to block vision. Use smokes to cover dangerous angles so your team can enter the site safely.
  • Sentinels (Killjoy, Cypher, Veto): Your job is the "Anchor." Place traps to watch the flank or stop the enemy from rushing your site.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common control-related questions

What's the best Fire Button size?

For most players, 45% to 55% is the sweet spot. If it's too big, you won't have space to "drag" for headshots. If it's too small, you'll miss the button during intense fights.

How do I fix "Aim Lock" on the chest?

If your aim keeps sticking to the enemy's chest, increase your General and Red Dot sensitivity by 5-10 points. This makes it easier to pull the crosshair up to the head for those crucial headshots.

Should I use the Gyroscope in 2026?

Yes! In 2026, many top-tier snipers use Gyroscope (moving the phone to aim). It takes about a week to learn but provides the most precise aim possible for long-range shots. Start with low sensitivity (20-30) and gradually increase.

What's the ideal button opacity?

Set opacity to 40-60%. This makes buttons visible enough to use but transparent enough to not block your view. Critical buttons like fire can be at 70-80% for better visibility.

The journey to the World Tree has introduced new legendary variants and raid bosses that define the end-game.

  • Bellanoir Libero: The current "magic meta" queen. She has the highest magic attack stat in the game (150) and can wipe out entire groups of enemies with her area-of-effect Dark skills.

Combat in 2026 is about synergy. You aren't just watching your Pal fight; you are fighting with them using Partner Skills.

  • The "Missile" Meta (Jetragon): Still the fastest flyer in the game. Its Aerial Missile partner skill allows you to rain down rockets while flying, making it the best for clearing Syndicate camps.

üß¨ Part 5: Pro-Tips for Pal Training

  • Passive Trait "Perfecting": The ultimate Pal has four specific traits: Legend, Musclehead, Ferocious, and Burly Body (for +70% total attack and +20% defense).
  • The Condenser: Don't delete your "weak" Pals. Use the Pal Condenser to merge 116 identical Pals into one "4-Star" version, which boosts their stats and upgrades their Work Suitability levels by +1.
  • Statue of Power: Always offer Pal Souls to the Statue of Power to increase your Pal's HP and Attack. In 2026, you can also use "Large Pal Souls" to reset a Pal's individual IV stats.

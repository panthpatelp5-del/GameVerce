Combat & Weaponry (2026 Arsenal)

New weapons and upgraded combat mechanics for 2026

The Spear (New for 2026)

Tiered Weapon

The first "tiered" weapon (Wood, Stone, Iron, Gold, Diamond, Netherite) added in years.

Pro Strategy: Features a unique Charge Attack. Hold the attack button for a thrust that deals massive damage. Damage is linked to speed - riding a Horse, Camel, or Nautilus gives a "Lance" bonus that can one-shot most mobs.

The Mace

High-Flying Combat
Damage Multiplier

Multiplies damage based on how far you fall before hitting the target. Perfect for surprise attacks from above.

Fall Damage Cancel

Successfully landing a hit completely negates fall damage. Great for drop attacks.

Tiered Armor & Wolf Armor

Defense

Wolf Armor can now be dyed and trimmed just like player armor, providing your tamed wolves with better protection.

Armor Trims: These decorative "Smithing Templates" from Trail Ruins and Ancient Cities allow you to add patterns of Gold, Diamond, or Amethyst to your gear.

Artifacts & Rare Treasures

Items that cannot be farmed easily and often define your "end-game" status

Totem of Undying

Game-Changer

Held in the off-hand. It saves you from death once. Essential for Hardcore players and difficult raids.

Why Use It

Can save you from certain death in boss fights or surprise attacks. Gives regeneration and absorption after activation.

Farming Tip

Best obtained from Woodland Mansions and Raid captains. Always keep one in your off-hand during dangerous activities.

Recovery Compass

Utility

Crafted with Echo Shards from Ancient Cities. Points to the location of your last death.

Why Use It

Essential for retrieving items after an unexpected death, especially in dangerous areas like the Deep Dark.

Multiplayer Note

Only tracks YOUR last death location. Each player needs their own compass.

Creaking Heart & Resin

Defensive

Gathered with Silk Touch from Pale Gardens. Summons "Creaking" protectors for your base.

Why Use It

Place between Pale Oak logs at night to summon automated base defenders that attack hostile mobs.

Resource

Resin Clumps can be smelted into vibrant orange Resin Bricks for unique building aesthetics.

üåÖ Phase 1: The "New Dawn" (Levels 1‚Äì15)

The tutorial has been streamlined, but the core objective remains: survive and expand.

  1. The Berry Meta:

    Don't just punch trees. Gather berries immediately. In 2026, hunger is slightly more punishing, but cooked berries provide a small Sanity (SAN) buff that keeps your early-game Pals from slacking.

  2. Capture Your "Worker Bees":

    Focus on catching 12 of each common Pal (like Cattiva and Lamball). This provides a massive XP bonus that will rocket you to Level 10 in minutes.

  3. The First Base:

    Place your Palbox in a wide, flat area. In 1.0, the Sakurajima Island coastline is a popular start for its proximity to early Crude Oil nodes.

  4. Blueprints over Levels:

    Look for Legendary Schematics in low-level chests. A "Legendary Old Bow" (found by defeating the Alpha Kingpaca) is often stronger than a standard Metal Crossbow.

üè≠ Phase 2: Industrial Mastery (Levels 16‚Äì35)

This is where the game moves from "Survival" to "Factory Management."

  • Automate Everything: Craft a Monitoring Stand. Use it to assign specific tasks to your Pals. In 2026, you can toggle off a Pal's "Lumbering" ability so they focus entirely on "Handiwork."
  • Breeding is Mandatory: You cannot find the strongest Pals in the wild early on. Build a Breeding Farm and start producing Anubis (the best builder) and Jormuntide Ignis (the best smelter).
    • Recipe: Breed a Bushy and a Penking to get an Anubis long before you can fight the level 47 boss version.
  • The Ore Crisis: At Level 20+, you will need thousands of Ore. Don't mine it yourself. Set up your second base on a plateau with at least 6 Ore nodes and fill it with Digtoise.

The 2026 end-game is defined by the Raid Battlefields and Tower Bosses.

  1. Raid Bosses:

    Collect Slab Fragments from high-level dungeons to summon bosses like Bellanoir Libero.

    • CRITICAL: Do NOT summon them in your main base. Build a dedicated "Raid Arena" (a Palbox and a flat floor) so they don't destroy your storage chests and machines.
  2. The "Ultrakill" Loadout:

    Use the new 2026 crossover weapons. The Marksman Revolver allows you to ricochet shots off coins for guaranteed critical hits on Bosses' weak points.

  3. Elemental Synergy:

    You can no longer brute-force bosses. If you are fighting Selyne (Dark/Moon), you must bring a team of Dragon-type Pals like Jetragon or Relaxasaurus to survive.

üå≥ Phase 4: The World Tree (The 1.0 Finale)

As of 1.0, the barrier around the World Tree has fallen. This is the ultimate challenge.

  • Ancient Tech: You need "Ancient Civilization Parts" from Alpha bosses to craft the Anti-Gravity Belt, which is required to navigate the floating islands surrounding the Tree.
  • Ancient Passive Traits: Pals caught in this region have "Ancient" traits that allow them to bypass 20% of an enemy's defense. These are the new "God-tier" Pals for PvP and Raids.

üí° 2026 Survival Tips & Tricks

  • The "Mercy" Rule: Always wear the Ring of Mercy. It ensures your attacks leave Pals at 1 HP, preventing you from accidentally killing a rare "Lucky" or "Alpha" Pal you are trying to catch.
  • Status Effect Stacking: In 1.0, stacking Poison and Burn is the meta for high-HP bosses. It deals percentage-based damage that ignores the boss's massive defense stats.
  • Statue of Power: Use your Pal Souls wisely. Focus on upgrading your "Base Pals" (Work Speed) first, and your "Combat Team" (Attack/Defense) second.

üîÑ 3. The "Breeze" 2026 Rework

Breeze returned to the pool this month with significant changes aimed at reducing the "Viper-only" meta.

  • Tighter Chokes: The "A-Main" and "Mid-Pillar" areas have been narrowed with extra crates and debris. This allows dome-smokers like Brimstone and Clove to effectively cover sightlines that previously required a Viper wall.
  • Hall Tunnel: The infamous "Tube" has been simplified, removing some of the verticality to make it a more predictable flanking route.

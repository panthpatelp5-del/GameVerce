Part 1: The "Pale Garden" & 2026 New Arrivals

Master the latest biome additions and rare materials

Pale Oak Wood

A ghostly, white-gray wood set with a "desaturated" look. Perfect for gothic or modern minimalist builds.

Rarity: Uncommon NEW

Resin Clumps & Blocks

Found within Pale Garden trees. Craft into bright orange, high-gloss blocks resembling plastic or amber.

Rarity: Rare 2026

The Creaking Heart

A "living" block found inside Pale Oak trees. Destroying it stops the Creaking mob from spawning in the area.

Rarity: Very Rare BIOME BOSS

Eyeblossoms

Flowers that open and close based on time of day. At night, they turn orange and emit a faint, spooky glow.

Light Level: 3-7 DYNAMIC

Mining Strategy

Pro Mining Techniques

Silk Touch vs Fortune: Use Silk Touch to collect ore blocks for decoration. Use Fortune III for maximum resource yield. Always carry both pickaxes.

Branch Mining: Mine at Y=-58 for diamonds. Create 1x2 tunnels 2 blocks apart. Use efficiency pickaxes for speed.

Part 4: Dimensional Blocks

Nether and End exclusive materials

Netherrack & Blackstone

Netherrack burns forever. Blackstone is the "Nether's Cobblestone" - perfect for dark, imposing castles.

Blast Res: Low NETHER

Soul Sand & Soul Soil

Slows movement. When lit, produces eerie Blue Flames. Essential for soul speed enchantment farms.

Speed: -50% SOUL

End Stone & Purpur

End Stone has high blast resistance. Purpur blocks give a royal, End-themed aesthetic to builds.

Blast Res: High END

Glowstone & End Rods

Glowstone provides bright, natural light. End Rods offer modern, directional lighting options.

Light: 15 LIGHTING

Part 5: Redstone & Functional Blocks

Automation, logic, and machinery components

Observers

Function: Block Update Detection

Detects changes in adjacent blocks (crop growth, block placement) and emits a redstone pulse.

Sculk Sensors

Function: Vibration Detection

Wirelessly detects vibrations (footsteps, eating, block placement) in 9-block radius. Wool blocks dampen vibrations.

Sticky Pistons

Function: Push/Pull Blocks

Essential for secret doors, elevators, and complex machinery. Regular pistons only push.

Crafter (2026 Meta)

Function: Auto-Crafting

Automatically crafts items when fed materials via hopper. Game-changer for mass production farms.
